Atmos

The OS that you can trust.

Atmos is a free and open-source operating system based on Arch Linux.

It uses the GNOME desktop environment.

It aims to:

Be a completely AI-free operating system.

Not sell your data to data brokers.

Treat you as an owner. not the product.
